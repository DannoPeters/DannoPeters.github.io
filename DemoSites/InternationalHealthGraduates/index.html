ABOUT US

 

International Health Graduates Inc. provides preparatory courses for internationally trained nurses, physicians, pharmacists, and physiotherapists looking to obtain a license to practice in Canada.

 

International Health Graduates Inc. also provides professional services including interview training, curriculum vitae preparation, as well as assistance with other document preparation in order to provide our clients with the highest likelihood of success in obtaining Canadian license in with their respective health profession.

 

Owned and operated by experienced practicing physicians and nurses, International Health Graduates Inc. provides you your best opportunity for success on professional evaluation exams.

 

 

OUR TEAM

 

International Health Graduate Consultants Inc. is owned and operated by experienced licensed practicing physicians and nurses.

 

Course instructors are fully qualified, practicing, experienced physicians, registered nurses, pharmacists, and physiotherapists.

 

Curriculum vitae development, and interview training is overseen by experienced human resource (HR) professionals, and fully qualified English as second language (ESL) instructors.
