The Saskatchewan Aviation Historical Society Inc. is a non-profit, charitable organization founded and operated by volunteers to preserve Saskatchewan's strong Aviation past.

 

 

Our Museum is located in Hangar 5 at the John G Diefenbaker International Airport in Saskatoon

The Saskatchewan Aviation Historical Society restores and conserves aircraft to act as technological records of the past and  help to keep skills and manufacturing techniques from vanishing.

 

We work with the Saskatchewan Aviation Council (SAC), Canadian Aeronautical Preservation Association (CAPA), and belong to the Museums Association of Saskatchewan (MAS).

 

We are the caretakers of Saskatchewan aviation history, documenting and preserving photographs, techniques, artifacts, and stories.
